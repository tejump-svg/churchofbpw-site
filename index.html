Purpose

The Church of the BPW is a religion organized around a formal moral model. It defines moral evaluation using a single principle: whether a state of affairs involves involuntary imposition on the will of a moral patient.

The BPW Standard

BPW refers to the Best Possible World or Best Possible Worlds: a moral reference standard in which no conscious will is subjected to involuntary imposition.

The singular denotes the ideal moral limit. The plural reflects that such a condition may require multiple, non-interacting domains.
